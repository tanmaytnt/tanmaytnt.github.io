My ideal job would be a perfect balance between economical and mental satisfaction. This means that i must be able to have fun at my job while also earning to enough to fulfil all my desires.

After due consideration i found out my ideal job to be anAPPLICATIONS ARCHITECT

An applications architect :

  • Analyzes business needs and customer problems, establish technical requirements, and build end-to-end solutions
  • Works with clients, vendors, engineering, and support teams to create project alignment and find cost effective solutions.
  • Provides technical leadership to the application development team.
  • Designs major aspects of the architecture of an application, including components such as user interface, middleware and infrastructure.
  • Collaborates with other stakeholders to ensure the architecture is aligned with business requirements
